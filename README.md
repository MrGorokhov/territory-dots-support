Territory Dots is a modern strategy game inspired by the classic paper-and-pen dots battles.

Place dots, build borders, surround your opponent, and capture territory. Every move matters. A single mistake can open space for your rival, while a well-timed enclosure can turn the whole board in your favor.

Features

* Infinite board for open-ended strategy
* Play against AI
* Clean, minimal interface
* Territory capture with visible borders
* Undo, pass, resign, and restart controls
* Fast matches, deep tactics

Why Territory Dots

* Easy to learn
* Pure strategy, no luck
* Inspired by the classic notebook game
* Designed for quick play and long-term mastery

Whether you remember this style of game from paper notebooks or you are discovering it for the first time, Territory Dots gives you a focused tactical experience built for modern mobile play.

If you like abstract strategy games, territory control, and smart turn-based battles, Territory Dots is for you.
