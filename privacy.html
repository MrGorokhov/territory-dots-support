Privacy Policy

Territory Dots is a simple strategy game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Territory Dots does not require account creation.

Territory Dots does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location from within the app.

The app may store local gameplay data on your device, such as game state or preferences, in order to support normal app functionality.

This data stays on your device unless you choose to share it through your device or platform features.

Analytics and Tracking

Territory Dots does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Territory Dots does not sell your personal data.

Third-Party Services

If a future version of the app adds third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, or online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

Children’s Privacy

Territory Dots is a general audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because Territory Dots is designed to work without collecting personal account data, the amount of user data handled by the app is minimal.
